How to Reach Shillong

By Air

Shillong Airport (Umroi) is approximately 35 km from the city center. Taxis are readily available. Shillong can be conveniently accessed by air via the Shillong Airport, a domestic airport catering to travelers heading to the captivating capital of Meghalaya, India. Nestled in Umroi, the airport is positioned approximately 35 km away from the bustling city center. Upon landing, you'll find reliable taxi services readily accessible, eager to transport you seamlessly to the heart of the state capital.

By Road

Shillong is about 100 km from Guwahati via NH-40. Private taxis and government buses operate regularly. Should you choose to take the road to Shillong, know that the drive is absolutely beautiful. Your journey may commence from Guwahati passing through the Jorabat junction on the National Highway 40. The journey is approximately 2 hours and 46 minutes from Guwahati. You can opt to hire a private taxi/shared cab from Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport or Guwahati Railway Station. There are government- operated MTC and ASTC deluxe buses that ply regularly from Guwahati.
