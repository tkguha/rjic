About North-Eastern Hill University

Established in 1973 by an Act of the Indian Parliament, the North Eastern Hill University (NEHU) is a prominent institution in Shillong, India. It stands as a central institution for promoting education and research in the northeastern region of India. The dual-campus structure in Meghalaya, with locations in both Shillong and Tura, enables the university to cater to a broader base of students and researchers, fostering regional development and accessibility to education.
