Envisaged Participants

  • Practicing professionals including librarians, knowledge management professionals, and information specialists working across academic, public, and special library sectors, who are engaged in the organization, management, and dissemination of information.
  • Academics, researchers, educators, and students from the field of Library and Information Science, as well as from related disciplines, who are committed to advancing scholarship, pedagogy, and interdisciplinary collaboration in the domain of information studies.
