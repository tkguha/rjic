Ruby Jubilee International Conference

Celebrating 40 Years of Excellence

The Ruby Jubilee International Conference focuses on the multifaceted role of information, knowledge systems, technologies, and libraries in various socio-cultural and academic environments.

Organized by the Department of Library and Information Science, North-Eastern Hill University, the conference brings together librarians, researchers, academicians, and professionals.

Dates: 26–27 February 2026

Venue: NEHU, Shillong, Meghalaya
